Frequently Asked Questions

Our platform has a standard cancellation policy: if a chef cancels more than 48 hours before the booking, they receive a full refund. If they cancel between 24-48 hours, they receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 24 hours receive no refund. You can view and manage your cancellations in the Bookings tab.

Payments are processed weekly every Monday for all completed bookings. The platform retains a 15% service fee, and the remaining 85% is transferred to your designated payout account. You can view your upcoming and past payouts in the Earnings tab.

All chefs are required to follow the kitchen rules and clean up after use. In the rare case of damage, our platform has a protection policy. Document the damage with photos and submit a claim through the Support tab within 24 hours of the booking end. Our team will review the claim and assist with the resolution.

You can manage your kitchen's availability calendar in the Availability tab. Block off dates when your kitchen is unavailable, set regular business hours, and handle specific time slots. Changes to availability won't affect existing confirmed bookings.

When you receive a booking request, you'll get an email and SMS notification. You can approve or decline the request from your Dashboard or Bookings tab. You have 12 hours to respond before the system auto-approves the booking. We recommend responding promptly to provide excellent service to potential customers.
